HTML Entities

Character entities are used to display reserved characters in HTML.HTML entities are used for special characters or symbols in html document.They are not present in your standard keyboard.They provide a wide range of charactetrs,allowing you to add icons,geometric shapes,mathematical operators,and so on.Each of these codes starts with an ampersand(&),and ends with a semicolon.

symbols-

1. ©(copyright)
2. £(pound)
3. ¥(yen)
4. €(euro)
5. ¢(cent)
6. $(dollar)
